About

Mallige, a flower crop grown commercially in coastal Karnataka belonging to the family Oleaceae, means fragrant. Due to its gentle smell, which imparts feelings of optimism, happiness & confidence, Mallige is widely utilized on religious occasions. As per the Hindu article dated on 05th May 2021, it is found that there are around 8000 jasmine cultivators in the Mangalore district whereas post covid, the migrants from Mumbai to Mangalore producing Mallige flowers have been in large numbers. The research project aims to encompass the localites of Mallige producers from two districts of Karnataka state namely Mangalore, Udupi, and its interior villages Kateel, Mudarangadi, Kodettur, and Shirva evaluating a CPM (Critical Path Method), building a smooth logistics network for delivering mallige flowers from Mangalore to Mumbai markets through a digitized portal carving a niche in the flower market.
