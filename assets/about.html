About Mallige


Mallige, a flower crop grown commercially in coastal Karnataka belonging to the family Oleaceae, means fragrant. Due to its gentle smell, which imparts feelings of optimism, happiness & confidence. Mallige is widely utilized on religious occasions such as While worshiping God, for decoration in Marriages and it also used in weddings in the Karnataka region, irrespective of religion, Mallige strings are used to decorate the bride. It holds religious significance in Tulunadu, Bunts nad South Indian Culture.
In metropolitan city market, there are various flowers which are culturally and commercially important such as Mogra, Rose, Chafa, Marigold, Lotus etc. But Mallige Flower is one of the highly demanded, high price and rare flower. It's demand is higher due to Bunts, Tulunadu and South Indian population in metropolitan city. The population is nearby 45000 - 50000 families who buy Mallige on daily or occasionally basis. Mallige daily sell is approx 80 to 100 attis in metropolitan city on daily basis.
